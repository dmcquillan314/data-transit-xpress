3 Logos

The Peapod logo is the cornerstone of our brand identity. Whenever and wherever it appears, it should always be clear and visible. Care should be taken in controlling the background on which it is presented in order to optimize legibility.

3.1 Peapod Logo

Usage

To maximize readability, it is strongly recommended that the Peapod logo only appear on solid backgrounds such as white. Avoid using busy backgrounds such as photos, illustrations or patterns. Do not use colors outside of the approved color palette. Do not add bounding boxes or other graphic elements to the lock-up. Do not use blends, drop shadows, filters or effects. Official logos should never be adjusted in any way other than scaled to fit the media.

Clear Space

Clear space is the minimum “breathing room” maintained around the brandmark. It should be kept free of graphics, text and other marks. The clear space is equal to the height of a lowercase letter (x-height) from the logo.
